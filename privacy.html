Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect and manage your data at ATHNA.

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

Information We Collect

To process your orders and provide the best possible service, we collect the following personal information:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone numbers, and shipping addresses.
  • Conversation Data: Order details and shipping information exchanged via WhatsApp conversations, as this is our primary method of communication and order processing.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website and services.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, and operating system for analytical purposes.

Note on Cookies: We do not use cookies on our website to track personal data.

How We Collect Your Information

We collect information through the following means:

  • WhatsApp Conversations: When you contact us to place an order or ask a question, we collect the information you provide (name, phone number, shipping address) directly from our WhatsApp conversation. This is our primary method for collecting order-related data.
  • Direct Contact: When you email us or otherwise get in touch for customer support.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our store:

  • WhatsApp: Used for all customer communication, order placement, and shipping address collection. Your conversations are subject to WhatsApp's privacy policy.
  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website, helping us improve your experience. This service may collect anonymous usage data and device information.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, or if you wish to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data, please contact us:

Return & Shipping Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

Products & Shipping

We sell physical items including casualwear, sportswear, caps, hats, and accessories.

  • Shipping Locations: We ship nationwide within Indonesia and offer international shipping.
  • Carriers: We use JNE, SiCepat, and Pos Indonesia for domestic orders, and DHL or Pos Indonesia for international orders.
  • Processing Time: Orders are processed within 1-3 business days.
  • Shipping Costs: Calculated at checkout based on weight and destination. Free shipping is available for orders over Rp 1,000,000 IDR.

14-Day Return Window

  • Return Window: You have 14 days from the delivery date to request a return.
  • Condition: Items must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging with all tags attached.
  • Return Shipping: The customer is responsible for paying return shipping costs unless the item is defective or an error was made on our part.

Refunds & Exchanges

  • Refund Method: Refunds are issued to the original payment method (bank transfer, e-wallet) within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the return.
  • Exchanges: We offer exchanges for a different size or color, subject to availability. Contact us to arrange an exchange.
  • Store Credit: Store credit can be issued upon request as an alternative to a refund.

Exceptions

  • Final Sale: Items marked as "Final Sale" or purchased during clearance events cannot be returned or exchanged.
  • Personalized Items: Any customized or personalized items are non-returnable.

Need Help? Start a return by contacting us on WhatsApp at +62 878-6485-3508 or email cs@athna.store. Please have your order number ready.
